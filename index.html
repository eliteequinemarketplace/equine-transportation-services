Show Horse Transportation — WEF, WEC & Major Circuits

The Florida winter circuit season creates enormous demand for show horse transportation — horses traveling from across North America to Wellington and Ocala for the January through April season. Experienced show horse transporters who understand the care requirements of high-value competition horses, the timing pressures of the show circuit, and the specific needs of hunter jumper horses in transit are among the most sought-after transport professionals in the market. Elite Equine Marketplace lists show horse transport specialists serving the major hunter jumper circuits.

Interstate Horse Shipping — What to Know

Interstate horse transportation requires a current Coggins test (negative EIA test within the required timeframe), a Health Certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian within 30 days of transport for most states, and compliance with each state's entry requirements. Always confirm current health documentation requirements with your veterinarian and transport company before shipping across state lines.
